Notes & how to update

• Last year's applicant-type composition is summed from the daily category columns and may differ slightly (~2%) from the running total due to manual entry in the source sheet.
• This year's applicant total (228) = 159 on-campus + 69 online. Last year did not separate an online channel.
• Several dates in last year's file were stored ambiguously (e.g. 1/8 read as Jan-8); these were corrected back to day/month and validated against the weekday column.
• To refresh: re-run the extraction step against the updated Excel files to regenerate the embedded data block, then re-push to GitHub.

Notes & definitions

• To date = the snapshot at the same calendar date (late June) in each year — the fair pace comparison. Full semester = the latest cumulative total for each cycle (final numbers for completed years).
• Applicants resolve into Registered (new) + Unregistered + Drop All + Still Applicant.
• Total Registered = new Regular + new Transfer + continuing students.
• % Applicant→Student = applicants who progressed beyond “still applicant”. % Registered→Applicant = new registered ÷ applicants.
